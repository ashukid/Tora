Tell me about Jantar Mantar
The Jantar Mantar monument in Jaipur, Rajasthan is a collection of nineteen architectural astronomical instruments built by the Rajput king Sawai Jai Singh II in 1734. It features the world's largest stone sundial, and is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
